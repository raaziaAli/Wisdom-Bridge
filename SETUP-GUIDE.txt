║         WISDOMBRIDGE — AUTOMATIC SIGNUP STORAGE              ║
║         Every signup → your Google Sheet → welcome email     ║

Total time: about 20 minutes
No coding required. Follow each step exactly.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PART 1 — CREATE YOUR GOOGLE SHEET (5 minutes)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Go to: https://sheets.google.com
   (sign in with your Google / Gmail account)

2. Click the big "+" button to create a new spreadsheet

3. Name it at the top: "WisdomBridge Members"

4. Click on cell A1 and type these column headers
   exactly as shown, one per cell across row 1:

   A1: Timestamp
   B1: Name
   C1: Email
   D1: Age
   E1: Phone
   F1: Country
   G1: Role
   H1: Expertise
   I1: Open To
   J1: Bio / Career
   K1: Source

5. Make the header row bold:
   - Click the "1" on the left to select the whole row
   - Press Ctrl+B (or Cmd+B on Mac)

6. Leave this tab open — you'll need it in Part 2.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PART 2 — SET UP MAKE.COM (10 minutes)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Make.com is the free "bridge" between your website form
and your Google Sheet. It listens for signups and
automatically adds them as new rows.

STEP 1 — Create your free Make account
  → Go to: https://make.com
  → Click "Sign up for free"
  → Sign up with your email (free plan is enough)

STEP 2 — Create a new Scenario
  → On your Make dashboard, click "Create a new scenario"
  → You'll see a blank canvas with a "+" button

STEP 3 — Add the Webhook trigger (listens for signups)
  → Click the "+" button
  → Search for "Webhooks" and click it
  → Choose "Custom webhook"
  → Click "Add" then click "Save"
  → Make shows you a webhook URL — it looks like:
    https://hook.eu1.make.com/abcd1234efgh5678
  → COPY THIS URL — you need it in Part 3

  → Click "OK" then click "Run once"
    (Make is now waiting for a test signup)

STEP 4 — Test it (so Make learns your data structure)
  → Open your WisdomBridge website in another tab
  → Fill in the signup form and submit
  → Go back to Make — it should show "1 bundle received"
  → Click on the bundle to see your test data ✓

STEP 5 — Add Google Sheets action (saves each signup)
  → Click the "+" after your webhook bubble
  → Search "Google Sheets" and click it
  → Choose "Add a Row"
  → Click "Create a connection" → sign in with Google
     (allow Make to access your Google Sheets)
  → Under "Spreadsheet ID" — search for "WisdomBridge Members"
  → Under "Sheet Name" — select "Sheet1"
  → Now map each field:

     Column A (Timestamp)  → select "timestamp" from the list
     Column B (Name)       → select "name"
     Column C (Email)      → select "email"
     Column D (Age)        → select "age"
     Column E (Phone)      → select "phone"
     Column F (Country)    → select "country"
     Column G (Role)       → select "role"
     Column H (Expertise)  → select "expertise"
     Column I (Open To)    → select "open_to"
     Column J (Bio)        → select "bio"
     Column K (Source)     → select "source"

  → Click "OK"

STEP 6 — Add welcome email (optional but recommended)
  → Click "+" after the Google Sheets bubble
  → Search "Gmail" and click it
  → Choose "Send an Email"
  → Connect your Gmail account
  → Fill in:

     To:       {{email}}    ← click the field, select "email"
     Subject:  Welcome to WisdomBridge, {{name}}!
     Content:  (copy the email template from the bottom
                of this file)

  → Click "OK"

STEP 7 — Turn your Scenario ON
  → At the bottom left, flip the toggle from OFF → ON
  → Click "Save" (floppy disk icon)

  ✓ Your automation is now LIVE.
    Every signup goes straight to your Google Sheet
    AND sends a welcome email — automatically, forever.

PART 3 — CONNECT YOUR WEBSITE (3 minutes)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Now tell your website where to send signups.

1. Open the file: index.html  (in a text editor)

2. Near the top of the <script> section, find this line:

   const MAKE_WEBHOOK_URL = 'PASTE_YOUR_MAKE_WEBHOOK_URL_HERE';

3. Replace PASTE_YOUR_MAKE_WEBHOOK_URL_HERE with your
   actual Make webhook URL from Step 3 above. Example:

   const MAKE_WEBHOOK_URL = 'https://hook.eu1.make.com/abcd1234efgh5678';

4. Save the file.

5. Re-upload / redeploy to Netlify:
   → Go to your Netlify dashboard
   → Drag your updated folder onto the deploy area
   → Done — live in 30 seconds.

6. TEST IT:
   → Open your live website on your phone
   → Fill in the form and submit
   → Open Google Sheets — your entry should appear
     within about 5 seconds ✓
   → Check your email — welcome message should arrive ✓

Problem: Data not appearing in Google Sheets
Fix: Check that your Scenario in Make is turned ON (green)
     Check that the webhook URL in index.html is correct
     Try submitting the form again and watch Make's history

Problem: "Error" message on the form
Fix: Usually means the webhook URL is wrong
     Copy it again from Make → paste carefully into index.html

Problem: Welcome email going to spam
Fix: Ask your first few members to mark it "Not spam"
     Consider using a custom domain email later

Problem: Make free plan limit reached
Fix: Free plan allows 1,000 operations/month
     1 signup = 2-3 operations (webhook + sheets + email)
     So ~300-400 signups before needing to upgrade ($9/mo)
